About Hyper

Hyper's mission is to unlock the digitalization of the Industry.

Hyper is the data layer for the Industry, bridging the physical infrastructure with powerful analytics and automation tools for companies to build efficient, robust and sustainable industrial processes. We abstract complexity and deliver a simple, developer-friendly data platform with transparent pricing.

Values

Customer focus

Our customers are solving some of the most important problems in the world. They understand the industry better than we do. We help them by listening to their needs, removing obstacles and giving them powerful tools to be effective. We build trust and form long-term relationships by demonstrating our commitment and delivering consistent results.

Pragmatism

We solve problems that matter to our customers in a quantifiable way. We deliver fast and iterate. If a problem requires a complex solution, we break it down and focus on essentials. We aim to get 80% of results with 20% of effort. Perfection is not a goal, it's a process.

Simplicity

Industrial processes can be excessively complex. Managing complexity and abstracting it from our customers is our job. We focus on building small and composable building blocks that can be assembled into solutions. We design tools and protocols that are human-centered and easy to understand.

Teamwork

We are team of highly passionate, curious and dedicated people. We respect each other, we learn from each other and help each other grow. We value diversity of ideas and incorporate different opinions and perspectives when we solve problems. Disagreement is expected and being humble is encouraged.
